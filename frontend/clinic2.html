Visit Preparation

  1. Book an appointment online or by phone beforehand.
  2. Bring your valid student ID card.
  3. Have your insurance card or payment method ready.
  4. Arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time to fill out forms.
  5. List all medications you are currently taking.
